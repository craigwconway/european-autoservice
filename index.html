Who We Are

A Tradition of Excellence Since 1991

European Autoservice is a family owned and operated business with over 50 years of experience in service and repairs of European and Japanese automobiles, including diesel vehicles. Since in 1991, our facility has maintained the highest standards of excellence in service of fine European automobiles - specifically Audi, VW, BMW, Mercedes Benz, and Volvo. We also provide service and repair for all Japanese vehicles.

What We Do

Automotive Maintanence and Repairs

European Autoservice provides high quality service and maintanence for your new or vintage automobile. At our Walnut Creek Service Center, we are known for our high level of expertise regarding all models of Audi, VW, BMW, Mercedes Benz, and Volvo. Our trained technicians listen to you, and respect your time and budget. We only use the highest quality parts required for the maintanence or repair of your vehicle. Our professional services also extend to Japanese automobiles, including Lexus, Acura, Honda, Toyota, Subaru, and Nissan. Additionally, automotive sales and shipping services are available through our affiliate, Ata Car.
